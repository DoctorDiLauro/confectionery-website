about

  • Our history

    We started out just two, making our dark-chocolate for friends on special occasions. As time went on, friends spread the word of our work to others, and demand started to grow. We went from our garage to a small shop in Wellington West, and hired our first employee, my brother. Slowly, we were able to work less at our soul destroying office jobs, and spend more time on our passion. We are now 5, in our current location in Centertown, experimenting with new creations everyday, to bring you a chocolate that is anything but normal.

  • Our chocolate

    We use nothing but the best ingredients, ensuring that our cocoa is fair trade from sustainable sources. Everything else that goes into our chocolate we get locally whenever we can. We believe in experimenting, and trying things well outside the norm, in hopes to allow our customers to experience something new. We don't want to make something ordinary, traditional, and familiar, and hope to keep adding more types of chocolate to our ever expanding line-up.
